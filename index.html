Bio

James Vilho is currently a PhD student in the School of Electronics and Information Engineering at HIT Shenzhen since September 2023.

I earned my MEng degree from Nankai University in 2023, under the guidance of Dr. Mengting Hu. Prior to this, I got my master’s and bachelor degree from UPSI Malaysia in 2019 and 2017 respectively.

His research interests are at the intersection of machine learning and robotics, with a particular focus on sensor fusion data, deep representation learning, generative models and sensori-motor control in the context of autonomous systems.

His paper Autonomous Driving Planning based on Large Language Model: Collaborative Driving is accepted by 2025 IEEE 101st Vehicular Technology Conference!.
