Vilho James


I am a Ph.D. student (since 2023) in Information & Communication Engineering at the Guangdong Provincial Key Laboratory of Space-Aerial Networking & Intelligent Sensing , Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen , advised by Prof. Zhang Tingting .

Research: My research lies at the intersection of machine learning and robotics, with a particular focus on sensor fusion, deep representation learning, computer vision, and sensorimotor control for autonomous systems.

Bio: I received my M.Eng. in Software Engineeringfrom Nankai University in 2023, where I was advised by Prof. Mengting Hu.
